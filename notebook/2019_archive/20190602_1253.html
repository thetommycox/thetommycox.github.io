aeropress

lib's market
sumatra, dark-ish

24g coffee, 240g water, diluted with 70g water
1+12 on lido e-t
water at 192 deg f

inverted method
55g bloom for 30s with turbulent wiggle
at 30s, pour to 240g in 30s
at 1m, let brew for 3m
at 4m, invert and press for roughly 20s
4m 33s total brew time
dilute with 70g water at 176 deg f

overall impressions: slightly bitter, strong and very smooth - way better than i expected
honestly this one was just a test to see if i could do a coarse aeropress dark roast
eight stars out of ten
