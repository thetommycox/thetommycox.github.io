aeropress

cafe tres generaciones
costa rica peaberry, medium

20g coffee, 186g water
1+2 on lido e-t
water at 200 deg f

inverted method
45g bloom for 35s with slight turbulent wiggle
at 30s, pour to 186g
finish pour and let steep until 1m
at 1m, invert into mug and plunge
froth 150g milk in french press and pour over aeropress shot
