aeropress

proud mary
brazil, mantiqueira de minas - natural

15g coffee, 220g water
1+2 on lido e-t
water at 180 deg f

220g brew for 1m - turbulent wiggle for 30s
wet filter and cap at 30s, let steep
at 1m, invert over mug and press for ~30s
1m 37s total brew time

overall impressions: one of the best straight aeropress brews i've made in a minute
bold flavors, but absolutely zero acidity and negligible bitterness
puck looked a little non-uniform - need to experiment with stirring instead of wiggling
eight and three-quarter stars out of ten
